Edyt

Visit Website

During the last three years, I’ve served as the lead designer at Edyt and gatekeeper for anything that design produced. eDossier, perhaps one of the world’s largest government software projects in the world, offered an end-to-end solution for a patent examiner’s traditionally paper-based workflow. eDossier allowed patent examiners to receive applications, annotate on documents, map relationships between different documents and sections of documents, draft responses to applicants, and handle all business operations within the organization (such as handling time-off and assigning work to different employees).

Toward the end of my time at Edyt, I was also tasked with taking everything we had learned over the years and starting over outside the confines of studio-client relations. Below, you will notice a shift in layout and aesthetics, this is where that happened.
