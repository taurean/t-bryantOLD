Mac OS Dark Mode

MacOS Darkmode was a personal experiment in practicing visual design so that I could learn the interface design tool Figma. At the time, there was speculation that Apple might release a more advanced dark-mode to merge with night-shift. I wanted to explore what that might look like with a couple of Apple’s stock apps.
