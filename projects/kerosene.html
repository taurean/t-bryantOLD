Kerosene

Visit Website

On the various projects I’ve worked on over the past couple years, I began to notice myself tediously crafting the same visual elements (such as shadows) and applying the same techniques for tasks in Sass. That experience inspired me to craft a suite of SCSS tools to help expedite future work. Kerosene provides just the right amount of handholding without painting me into a corner or making it difficult to override things – that’s what makes it great, in my opinion. Eventually, Kerosene will be used to fuel a small set of components I will use for future Voyager projects under the name of Saturn V. Together with Kerosene fueling Saturn V (pun very much intended), they’ll form Apollo.
